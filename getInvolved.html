Get involved

Volunteer

Volunteers will be assigned to areas of work which most need support in AWAM, and which match their skills set. Volunteer positions are unpaid.

If you are interested to join us a volunteer, you may indicate your preferred area of work. We also accept volunteer writers, photographers, designers, and much more.


Who can be a volunteer?

  • Individuals aged 18 years old or above.
  • Individuals with strong integrity, are able to commit, and respect confidentiality.
  • ndividuals with good people skills—polite, gentle, and kind.
  • Individuals who can easily adapt to situations and are flexible enough to provide assistance whenever required.









What do volunteers do?

  • Assist public relation activities such as fundraising events
  • Utilise your specific skill sets to support the many specialised functions within AGN (e.g.: Research, IT, copy data entry).
  • Documenting events and meetings (written reports, photography, videography)
  • Assisting with the logistics and coord`ination of campaigns/events

    Children school supplies

    For kids growing up in impoverished communities, education is often their only chance to break poverty’s grasp. But poor learning environments and a lack of support cause many children to lose hope and drop out of school.

    What if you could give struggling kids access to the extra support they need? That’s where Children school supplies donation can make a difference for RM50 a month.

    Sponsor a child

    Your monthly gift of RM320 will give your sponsored child access to life-changing benefits, like medical care, educational support, life skills and job training before graduation.

    As a sponsor, you'll be able to see your impact firsthand through letters and photos from your child. You can even visit!

    E-Business

    The underpriviledged community will be able to have a source of income with an e-business. AGN uses your donation to provide training and workshops. These workshops help villagers gain valuable skills that they can build into a business

    Empowering mothers

    Your donation will also benefit single mothers and encourage them to pick up a skill for side-income.
