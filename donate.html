Donate now to AGN

AGN is a non-profit organisation, and we depend heavily on your generous support. All monies received are used strictly for running our programmes and sustaining our operations. If you believe in the work that we are doing, please consider making a donation. Your contribution could make a difference in someone’s life. No matter how small, it goes a long way.

Make a Pledge

Your pledge of monthly financial aid makes a difference to the life of others. You can make a difference. Your monthly donation will...

  • Improve nutrition and allow children to live healthier lives.
  • Allow families to afford food, school fees, and medicine for their children.
